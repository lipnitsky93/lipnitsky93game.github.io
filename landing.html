WarDraft

Play

When game start you have 100 health, and your enemy has 100 health too.

You have choise between two spells: attack and heal.

Then you must solve the task. The game has several types of the tasks.

If you put in the correct answer, you use spell, else monster attacks you.

When you kill monster new monster come instead.

The game continues until you lose all health.
